Hi, I'm Carly

I am an engineer by trade and a problem solver at heart.

I worked as a mechanical design engineer for a number of years, but decided to take a break through the COVID pandemic. My partner and I took this opportunity to grow our family, which gave me the luxury of time to discover my true interest - software development.

Many of the skills required to successfully deliver engineering projects are equally valuable in software development. As such, I bring with me enthusiasm, energy, and an arsenal of quality tools. I have liaised with clients; communicated to audiences with different levels of understanding; defined scope, budget and program for small to medium-sized projects.

From a technical perspective, I enjoy creating simple, elegant solutions to complex problems and delivering excellence. I love automation, testing and continuous improvement.

However, after working in a number of roles both in Australia and overseas, I have come to realise that the element common to all projects and roles is human connection. As such, whether the task is technical, commercial or process driven, my goal is to create a positive experience for the end user. As a result, I like to explore how my own task fits in the bigger picture, to ensure that my solution contributes to achieving this goal.

I am now excited to learn all I can about software development and share my experiences with others.
